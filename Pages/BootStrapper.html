Bootstrapping Simulator

A statistical python program that simulates multiple samplings over a small population

GitHub

Requires: Python2.6


A project for STAT451, to simulate statistical bootstrapping. In statistics, bootstrapping is when you re-sample over a small population to simulate a large population. My partner and I were skeptical that this method could produce accurate results so we made this program as a proof-of-concept using word lengths in a long book because we knew we could record the entire "population" of a book using a computer. What we discovered is that with very high amount of re-sampling (100k+) you could get a surprisingly accurate estimates of attributes about the population.
